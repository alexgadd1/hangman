Insturctions:

Player will select a letter from the alphabet, listed on the bottom of the screen. If letter selected is part of the word or phrase for the round, letter will be displayed in the blank spaces, and will be removed from the alphabet. If letter selected is not part of the word or phrase, the player loses a turn. Player will have a total of 7 guesses to spell out the letter. If the player is able to correctly spell the phrase or word in 7 guesses or less, the player wins the game.
